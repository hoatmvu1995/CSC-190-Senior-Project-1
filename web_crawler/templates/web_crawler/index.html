C.R.A.W.L

Introduction

Welcome to C.R.A.W.L. Configured Real-Time Application Web Lurker.

The purpose of this web application is to help Sac State users find information that's relevant to their needs on Reddit. This will help users who want to research more information from Reddit without having to through millions of posts is what “C.R.A.W.L” can do best.


Why use CRAWL?

Following ways you can use CRAWL:

  • A University trying to collect data from students participating in forums
  • A Professor curious about real, honest reviews about your classes
  • A Sac State student wondering about the difficulty of a certain class
  • Curious about Sac State News
  • Trying to report real-time posts about incidents on campus

    • More important aspects of CRAWL:

  • Different levels of CRAWL depending on account type of user. (ie. student, professor, administrator)
  • Advanced search functions are provided to all users to simplify their searches.
  • Varies on the user access level of CRAWL. (Higher level = most advanced functions available.) (Lower level = only basic advanced search functions available.)
  • Examples of Advanced search functions: From and To dates, search topics, additional keywords to simplify the general searches.
